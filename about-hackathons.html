What are Hackathons?

As the name may imply, a Hackathon is a Hacking Marathon - students formalize into teams to work together to solve a local issue, or, create a business, project, social movement or organization.

Fresh ideas are minted, discovered and curated in these sprint environments that enabling promising futures for participants, while establishing real world experience.

Networking Opportunities

This platform can connect you, and your student to talk to like-minded individuals interested in building meaningful change.

Skill Development

Take your skills further by practically showcasing it in a fast paced environment. Whether technical or business.

Prizes and Recognition

Organizations help incentivize students through prizes to create life changing ideas that may be used to genuinely impact other lives!

Career Opportunities

Sponsors, and organizations may find participants interested in leap starting their career through VC Funding or job careers!

Fun & Learning

This environment enhances and welcomes creativity and icebreaking to pour out innovative concepts and ideas.

Involvement credited

Participation in these events can be represented on resumes, portfolios, and through School ceremonies!

How to participate in your first hack:

1

Apply through our participation form

The greatest step you can take is just by applying to participate! You'll receive an email acknowledging your confirmation.

2

Come in with a positive mindset

Coming in with an open mind to learning and advancing yourself welcomes change and internal innovation!

3

Come in with your skillsets

As we formalize teams, it's always best to start your icebreakers off with your skillsets and how you can contribute to your team and projects vision!

4

Push yourself to try

It's as simple as that, apply yourself with determination and positivity throughout this hack and you'll see what you can truly accomplish.
